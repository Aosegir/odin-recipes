Hamburger

Description

A hamburger is a delicious meal consisting of a protein base between two slices of bread. The most popular additions to the hamburger include cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and some type of condiment, such as mayonnaise or ketchup. Sides usually accompany the hamburger, such as french fries. Clasically, the drink of choice is a milkshake of some kind.

Ingredients

  • Beef, 1/2 lb
  • Hamburger Buns, 2 Slices
  • Cheese, 2 Slices
  • Lettuce, 1
  • Tomato, 1
  • Onion, 1

Seasonings / Condiments

  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cook the meat, using salt, pepper, and butter to add flavor and make it easier to cook.
  2. Toast the buns, using butter.
  3. Assemble the burger, using the following list from bottom to top: bottom bun, meat, cheese, tomato, lettuce, onion, condiments, top burger bun.
